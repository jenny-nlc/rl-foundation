# policy-based

## taxonomy
* gradient-free
  * cross-entropy method
* gradient-based
  * TRPO: Trust Region Policy Optimization (Schulman, 2015)
  * DPG, Deep DPG, Recurrent DPG
* misc:
  * TNPG: Truncated Natural Policy Gradient
  * PILCO
  * GPS: Guided Policy Search, 2013
  * PEGASUS, 2000
  * DMP: Dynamic Motion Primitives
